Trek the Atlas Mountains with us

The Atlas Mountains stretch across Morocco, Algeria, and Tunisia, offering hiking and trekking experiences suited for all skill levels. Whether you’re scaling steep cliffs or strolling alongside tranquil streams and rivers, this region promises unforgettable adventures. For those eager to push further, climbing the highest peak, Toubkal, is an incredible challenge.

You can choose to climb with help from local porters, join a guided group tour, or explore the Atlas Mountains independently. The thrill of reaching the summit rivals the breathtaking views awaiting you there. Pair your mountain adventure with a Sahara desert experience—explore on foot or embark on a traditional camel ride. This is a must-visit destination for anyone dreaming of discovering the magic of North Africa!

Trekking routes for everyone

Easy

Our easy trekking tours are designed for all fitness levels, featuring short walking distances and low-altitude climbs. Many of these tours are family-friendly, welcoming children and travelers of all ages. No prior hiking experience is needed—if you’re comfortable walking long, flat distances, an easy hiking tour could be the perfect fit for you.

Moderate

Moderate hikes are ideal for those with a reasonable level of fitness, featuring longer walking days, steeper inclines, or slightly higher altitudes. While they don’t require intense preparation, you should be in good health, stay active, and exercise regularly to make the most of your hiking adventure.

Serious

A challenging trek demands strong physical and mental endurance, so thorough preparation is essential. These adventures often take you through remote, uninhabited regions where access to supplies is limited or unavailable. Serious hiking tours are best suited for experienced trekkers who are ready for a demanding and rewarding journey.
