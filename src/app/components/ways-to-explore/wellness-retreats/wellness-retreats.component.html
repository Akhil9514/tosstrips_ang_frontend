What to expect on a Wellness adventure

Explorer trips offer a “mix-and-match” experience, perfect for adventurers craving a little bit of everything. Want to tour iconic European cities? Check. Enjoy a sushi masterclass? Check. Cycle through charming towns? Check. Hike to catch a volcano sunrise? Check!

  • Nourish your body

    Whether you’re relaxing at a seaside resort or attending a yoga retreat high in the mountains, our Wellness adventures offer true peace of mind. They provide the perfect opportunity to nurture your body, embrace holistic healing, and take a well-deserved break from life’s hectic pace.

  • Rejuvenate your soul

    Make your Eat, Pray, Love dream a reality. Practice yoga at a serene monastery in Nepal, immerse yourself in the art of meditation in India, or unwind on a private tropical island in Thailand—wherever you choose to recharge, one thing is certain: it’s your time to focus on yourself.

  • Embrace ancient traditions

    For thousands of years, cultures around the globe have developed unique wellness traditions. Traveling offers the perfect opportunity to experience these authentic practices up close—whether it’s sipping Chinese herbal teas, enjoying traditional Indian Ayurvedic massages, or participating in South American cacao ceremonies.

  • Savour connections

    When we ground ourselves, we become our best selves with a clearer, calmer mind. Unwind and share your experience on a group wellness tour—whether it’s in the serene shadows of the Himalayas, beneath the shimmering Northern Lights, or after catching waves in Cancún.
