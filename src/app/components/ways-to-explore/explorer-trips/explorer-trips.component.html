What to expect on an Explorer adventure

Explorer trips offer a “mix-and-match” experience, perfect for adventurers who crave a little bit of everything. Touring iconic European cities? Check. Enjoying a sushi masterclass? Check. Cycling through charming villages? Check. Hiking up a volcano to catch the sunrise? Check!

  • Max. adventure, min. time

    We all love to travel, but sometimes time or vacation days just don’t stretch far enough. Explorer tours pack the most adventure into the shortest time, letting you experience new cultures, taste exciting foods, and get your adrenaline pumping—all in one unforgettable trip!

  • Good times, guaranteed

    Filled with a wide range of activities—from tracing the paths of ancient gladiators to camel rides across the Sahara—Explorer tours offer fun for the entire family. Whether you’re traveling solo, enjoying a romantic honeymoon, or celebrating an early retirement, an Explorer adventure is perfect for everyone!

  • A little bit of everything

    With so many incredible experiences waiting around the globe, why choose just one? On an Explorer tour, you don’t have to! Imagine sailing the Greek Islands, immersing yourself in ancient myths, and savoring fresh Mediterranean cuisine—all in one unforgettable adventure!

  • No regrets!s

    No other Adventure Style combines rich cultural experiences, bold new flavors, legendary stories, and secret spots even locals rarely discover. With a dash of active excursions to fully embrace the wonders of each destination, your Explorer tour will leave you craving your next adventure!

Your Explorer trip questions, answered

  • How do I know an Explorer tour is right for me?

    If you’re the kind of traveler who loves a little bit of everything on vacation, an Explorer tour is just right for you! Crafted to maximize your experience without breaking the bank, Explorer adventures are budget-friendly and the most popular Adventure Style on Tossntrip — and that says a lot!

  • Are Explorer tours fast-paced?

    Explorer tours tend to be more fast-paced compared to our other Adventure Styles, packing a variety of activities into a shorter timeframe. That said, you’ll still have plenty of free time to soak in your surroundings. If you prefer a slower, more immersive experience, our Travel Experts can connect you with operators who offer just that.

  • What’s the difference between a Cultural and Explorer tour?

    We’ve crafted Adventure Styles to match every traveler’s preference. If immersing yourself in diverse cultures—or even rediscovering your own—is what you seek, a Cultural tour is perfect for you! For those who crave a mix of experiences, our Explorer tours offer the best of both worlds—imagine exploring Andean hill tribes in Peru alongside a hike to Machu Picchu and encounters with llamas!

  • What if I don’t want to do an activity on tour?

    No worries—most activities on our tours are optional. Just reach out to our Travel Experts or let your guide know if you prefer to skip something. Often, your guide can suggest equally enjoyable alternatives, like sampling local dishes at charming cafes or taking some free time to explore at your own pace!

  • Can I combine Adventure Styles?

    Absolutely! Your adventure is yours to create—why not enhance it with wine tasting in France, island hopping through the Greek Islands, or an exhilarating safari in Kenya?

  • How do I book an Explorer tour on Tossntrip?

    Discover your next Explorer adventure by browsing our tours until you find the perfect fit. Just select your dates, complete your details, and lock in your trip. If you need any help, our Travel Experts are available around the clock to assist you!
