What to Expect from a Festivals & Events Package

  • How do I know an Festivals & Events Package is right for me?

    If you love discovering new and exciting destinations where no two days are the same, and enjoy activities that let you truly connect with the local culture and environment, then an adventure tour is perfect for you. Adventure tours come in various levels of difficulty, so whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned explorer, there’s an option to suit your pace!

  • How far in advance do I need to book?

    As with any adventure, booking early gives you the best chance of securing your spot, and festival tours are no exception. Since events often have limited tickets, if there’s a particular one you want to attend, it’s wise to reserve your place well in advance.

  • Will my adventure be full of young people?

    People of all ages come together to celebrate! Expect to meet a diverse group of travelers from around the globe. Some festivals, like the Sziget Festival, tend to attract younger crowds, while events such as ANZAC Day ceremonies or the Royal Ascot draw a wider age range. Ultimately, the best event is the one that feels right for you.

  • Is admission included?

    A dedicated Festival & Events adventure typically revolves around the festival or event itself, and sometimes the ticket is included in the overall price. However, since many festivals offer various ticket types with different perks and levels of access, admission is often excluded to give you the freedom to choose how you want to participate. If you’re unsure, don’t hesitate to contact our Customer Support team for clarification.

  • Can I combine Adventure styles?

    Absolutely! Your adventure is what you make it, so why not enhance it with wine tasting in France, island-hopping through the Greek Islands, or an exhilarating safari in Kenya?
