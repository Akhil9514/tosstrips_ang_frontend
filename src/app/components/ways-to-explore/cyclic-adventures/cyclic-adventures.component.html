Cycling Adventures

It’s time for a bicycle adventure! Ride off the beaten path through the rolling hills of Northern Thailand, glide past the fragrant fields of Provence, or savor the endless rows of vineyards in Tuscany after a rewarding day on the bike. Whether you prefer mountain biking or road cycling, a biking trip opens up a whole new world—whether you’re cruising sun-dappled trails in Vermont or tackling challenging climbs on Nepal’s iconic routes.

When exploring the world, there are countless ways to experience a city’s energy or the natural wonders of a national park—but few offer the connection and freedom of traveling by bike. If you want to make the most of your visit to Washington D.C., Zion National Park, or anywhere else across this vast country, consider a bicycle adventure in the US!

What to expect on a Bike Trip

  • Learn tricks of the trade

    Ride with a group or choose a private tour with a passionate cycling guide. Your guide is an experienced cyclist dedicated to making your journey smooth and enjoyable—offering tips from breathing techniques to pacing, all while ensuring everyone has a great time. By the end, you’ll part ways as close friends!

  • On your own, confidently

    If you like exploring at your own speed, a self-guided biking trip could be perfect for you. With an organized bicycle adventure, all the details are handled—from accommodations to navigation gear—so you can simply show up and enjoy the ride, knowing everything is taken care of.

  • Stays with a difference

    Some of the world’s best cycling routes wind along peaceful riverbanks and through charming villages where locals greet visitors with open arms. Many bicycle tours include local hosts who make sure you get plenty of rest and serve up homemade meals to fuel your ride the next day!

  • Connect over cycling

    The best part of any adventure is sharing it with someone special. Embark on your cycling journey with a fellow enthusiast—celebrate your victories, support each other through challenges, swap tips, and enjoy great food and company along the way.

Best ways to cycle

Mountain biking

Experience the perfect blend of tranquil, postcard-worthy landscapes and thrilling mountain bike adventures! Though the winding uphill trails can be tough, the rush of wind in your hair makes every challenge worthwhile. Whether you’re conquering the rugged, chilly slopes of Annapurna or the sun-baked hills of the High Atlas Mountains, a mountain biking adventure is sure to leave you exhilarated and inspired.

Road cycling

If off-the-beaten-path cycling isn’t your thing, why not try a Road Cycling adventure? Whether you’re pedaling past wildlife in Africa or winding through Europe’s historic streets, there’s a cycling holiday for every kind of traveler. Suitable for all ages and fitness levels—from the youngest riders to the eldest—road cycling trips offer something for everyone.

Bike & barge

What better way to unwind after a day of cycling than aboard a floating hotel? Spend your days pedaling through vineyards, past ancient castles, and along rivers winding through picturesque towns. From charming villages along the Seine to iconic cities lining the Danube, a Bike and Barge trip offers the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation.

Mountain biking

Experience the contrast of peaceful, postcard-perfect landscapes with the thrill of a mountain biking adventure! Though the steep, winding trails can be demanding, the rush of wind in your hair makes every effort worthwhile. From the icy, rugged peaks of Annapurna to the sunbaked hills of the High Atlas Mountains, a mountain biking journey will absolutely blow you away!

Your River Cruise trips questions, answered

  • When will I receive my travel documents?

    Once your cruise is paid in full, you can expect to receive your travel documents about 3–4 weeks before departure. These typically include your eTicket receipts, detailed itineraries, and other important information to help you prepare for your adventure.

  • When and where do I board the ship?

    Your final travel documents will include all embarkation details. Boarding generally begins at 3:00 PM, giving the crew time to prepare your stateroom and ensure the ship is ready for your journey. Please note that factors such as weather, water levels, and ship scheduling can affect the final docking location. For this reason, we recommend waiting until you receive your travel documents before booking any flights or accommodations.

  • What facilities will be in my room?

    Most staterooms come equipped with comfortable beds, towels, hairdryers, a writing desk, charging ports, and plenty of storage space. Some ships also offer onboard laundry facilities for an additional fee. Amenities may vary depending on the room and ship, so be sure to review the Ship Details for your specific adventure—or reach out to your TossnTrips Cruise Consultant for a full list of inclusions.

  • Are shore excursions and meals included?

    Most TossnTrips cruises include breakfast, lunch, and dinner served onboard, with only a few exceptions depending on the operator. Some river cruise operators also include shore excursions in the overall trip cost. For a detailed list of what's included in your cruise, please reach out to your TossnTrips Cruise Consultant.

  • Are transfers to and from the port included?

    Some river cruise operators include complimentary arrival and departure transfers as part of your tour package. If transfers aren’t included, please contact your TossnTrips Cruise Consultant to arrange and purchase airport transfers.

  • Can I take a wheelchair onboard?

    Some ships may not be fully accessible for travelers using wheelchairs, due to challenges with boarding and disembarking, the absence of onboard elevators, and narrow doorways. For those using a collapsible wheelchair, storage is typically limited to the stateroom, which may feel cramped. For more details, please reach out to your TossnTrips Cruise Consultant.
