Discover ancient worlds

Trace the paths of legendary figures from ancient times to recent history — from the tombs of Tutankhamun to the arenas where gladiators faced their fate before Julius Caesar. Awaken your inner Indiana Jones and embark on a thrilling journey into the past.

  • Unlock secrets of the past

    Stroll through castles that echo with scandalous stories from the past, or stand on battlefields where history was forever altered. Uncover secrets long forgotten and explore museum artifacts that reveal tales of struggle, triumph, and resilience.

  • Embrace a new way of life

    Step into a world unlike your own and immerse yourself in experiences rich with meaning. A cultural tour celebrates art, music, culinary treasures, and time-honored traditions from across the globe, offering you unforgettable insights you won’t find anywhere else.

    Plan your adventure to coincide with unforgettable celebrations of tradition. Release lanterns into the glowing skies of Chiang Mai or join the joyful burst of colorful gulal powders in Jaipur, honoring all that is good in the world. There’s no better way to experience a culture than by celebrating like a local.

Most popular destinations for a Cultural tour

India

Our most immersive Northern India tours combine heritage stays with authentic local experiences over 10 to 14 days. Enjoy life in beautifully restored royal residences such as Narain Niwas Palace, where peacocks roam lush gardens and intricate architecture surrounds you at every turn.

Nepal

Nepal’s cultural tours offer deep, authentic experiences that go far beyond typical sightseeing. Begin in Kathmandu’s historic medieval quarters, where you can learn traditional Thangka painting from Buddhist artists and join cooking sessions featuring cherished family recipes passed down through generations.

Morocco

Experience true cultural immersion on our food and heritage tours, where you’ll master traditional pastilla in Fes, savor camel burgers in Meknes, and cook with fragrant herbs in the Middle Atlas Mountains. These unique journeys often include visits to women’s cooperatives, offering meaningful insights into local life.

Peru

Peru’s cultural tours explore the rich blend of colonial and Inca heritage, starting in Lima’s UNESCO-listed historic district where guides reveal how Spanish architecture intertwined with indigenous roots. Travelers especially appreciate visits to Casa Aliaga, a colonial mansion continuously owned by the same family since 1535, offering a unique glimpse into Peru’s living history.

Italy

Southern Italy’s rich heritage shines on our cultural tours, especially amid Puglia’s unique landscapes. Stay close to Alberobello’s iconic cone-shaped trulli houses, explore Matera’s ancient cave dwellings, and marvel at the ornate Baroque churches of Lecce. The authentic local connections truly bring these journeys to life.

Tanzania

At the heart of East Africa, a vibrant blend of wildlife and culture awaits. Experience life with Tanzania’s hunter-gatherer tribes and deepen your knowledge of the Big Five. Spend nights beneath star-filled skies in an authentic boma, and discover the beautiful harmony between local communities and the natural world.
