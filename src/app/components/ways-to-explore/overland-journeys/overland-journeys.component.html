What to expect on an Overland trip

  • One-of-a-kind experiences

    Get face-to-face with Rwanda’s incredible wildlife, surf down Namibia’s striking terracotta dunes, explore the ancient Inca ruins of the Andes, or meet koalas and kangaroos in the Australian Outback. An overland journey is full of adventure and limitless possibilities!

  • A wilderness escape

    Escape the city and immerse yourself in nature! Experience the magic of the outdoors: countless stars lighting up the night sky, cozy campfire gatherings, wildlife encounters in their natural habitats, endless open landscapes stretching to the horizon, and a new challenge each day that you’ll conquer together as a team.

  • Uncover hidden gems

    From the shimmering salt flats of Uyuni to the sacred Uluru and the stark beauty of Deadvlei’s arid plains, an Overland tour offers unforgettable travel experiences. Exploring remote spots known only to locals, this true escape from everyday life has been thrilling adventurers for decades!

  • Plenty of quality time

    As life gets busier, the urge to slow down becomes stronger. Overland adventures, which can last weeks or even months, offer the perfect chance to take meaningful time for yourself. Explore new destinations, connect with like-minded travelers, or simply enjoy some well-deserved downtime on an unforgettable Overland journey.

Your overlanding questions, answered

  • How do I book an Overland trip on Tossntrip?

    Discover your next Overland adventure by browsing our tours until you find the one that catches your eye. Just select your dates, enter your details, and book your trip. If you have any questions, our Travel Experts are available 24/7 to assist you!

  • Is an Overland tour right for me?

    Love the outdoors and want to explore more than just one spot? If you enjoy road trips with a touch of adventure, crave a break from the daily grind, and want to fully immerse yourself in new surroundings, then an Overland Tour is just right for you.

  • Do I have to camp on an Overland trip?

    Since Overlanding is all about embracing the great outdoors, most trips include camping. Often, camping is the only accommodation option because you’ll be venturing into remote areas—but don’t hesitate! The star-filled skies in these untouched locations are truly breathtaking and a sight you won’t want to miss.

  • What differentiates a Safari tour from an Overland tour?

    Safari tours primarily center on wildlife viewing in their natural environments, offering various accommodation options and usually taking place in jeeps. In contrast, overland tours cover a wider range of regions and cultural experiences. These adventures often involve camping and long-distance travel in specially equipped vehicles, delivering a deeper, more immersive journey.

  • Do I need to be fit for an Overland trip?

    Most Overland trips include setting up tents, helping with meal prep, sitting for extended periods, and driving on rough terrain, so a moderate level of fitness is recommended to get the most out of the experience. If you’re unsure about the physical demands, feel free to reach out to our Travel Experts for guidance. Looking for more active adventures like ziplining through the jungle or refreshing dives? We’ve got those too!

  • Can I combine Adventure Styles?

    Absolutely! You can mix and match our Adventure Styles—why not finish your Overland tour with a relaxing beach getaway or a culinary adventure? Feel free to explore your options, and if you need any help, our Travel Experts are here to assist!
