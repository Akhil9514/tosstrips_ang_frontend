Active trips for everyone

  • Easy

    Staying active doesn’t have to involve scaling mountains or rushing through rapids. Many adventure tours offer gentle experiences like easy hikes, calm kayaking or rafting, and brief zip line rides over vibrant forests. Adventure tours aren’t just for adrenaline junkies—they’re perfect for anyone looking to enjoy nature at a comfortable pace!

  • Moderate

    Designed for those with a moderate level of fitness, moderately active tours offer a variety of activities. You might enjoy kayaking trips, snorkeling to explore underwater wildlife, or wild overland adventures across the African savannah. To make the most of your moderately active journey, it’s important to be healthy, reasonably fit, and maintain a regular exercise routine!

  • Serious

    Now things get truly thrilling! If conquering towering peaks and racing down wild rapids sounds like your kind of adventure, this is the perfect fit. You’ll be crossing rugged mountain ranges, tackling challenging conditions, and soaking in some of the world’s most stunning natural landscapes. These active pursuits demand a strong level of fitness to fully enjoy the experience!

Your Adventure Tour Questions—Answered

  • How can I tell if an adventure tour is the right fit for me?

    If you love traveling to exciting new places, trying something different each day, and diving into experiences that truly connect you with your destination, then an adventure tour is a great choice for you. Keep in mind, adventure tours come in varying levels of difficulty—so whether you’re just starting out or already an experienced explorer, there’s a perfect option waiting for you!

  • Are activities part of the tour package?

    Adventure tours are all about the experiences, so most activities are typically included in the overall cost. Many operators also offer optional extras for those who want to take things up a notch and make the most of every moment. To see the full list of optional activities available on your trip, just send an inquiry—your tour operator will happily share all the details.

  • Do I need to train for an Active tour?

    Only if you want to! One of the best parts about booking your adventure tour with tossntripsis the flexibility to choose your own level of challenge—from easygoing walks to high-energy thrills. Just use our filters to find a tour that suits your style. Not sure where to start? Reach out to us—we’re here to help you find the adventure that’s just right for you.

  • What’s the difference between an Active tour and an Explorer tour?

    Our adventure styles are designed to suit all types of travelers, making it easy to find your perfect fit. If you enjoy a mix of experiences while exploring a new destination—like sightseeing, cultural visits, and local food—an Explorer tour is a great choice. Prefer a bit more action? Active tours include more physically engaging activities, such as ziplining through the jungle or diving in for a snorkel to cool off.

  • What about travel insurance?

    Travel insurance is required for most tours booked through tossntrips and must be arranged before your journey begins. It provides essential coverage for things like trip cancellations, medical emergencies, and lost or stolen baggage. This is especially important for adventure tours, where unexpected situations can arise. In many cases, you can conveniently add travel insurance from Cover Genius directly when making your tossntrips booking.

  • How can I book an adventure tour on TossnTrips?

    Discover your next adventure by browsing our tours until you find one that sparks your travel spirit. Use filters like destination, dates, key sights, and difficulty level to narrow down your options. Once you’ve made your choice, enter your details to book your trip. If you have any questions, just send an inquiry—the tour operator will get back to you with all the answers you need.
