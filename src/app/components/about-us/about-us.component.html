About Us

Our Origin

Brothers Travis and Shawn Pittman left Queensland, Australia, to live and work in London, UK, where their experiences opened their eyes to the transformative power of travel.

Like many startup founders, their idea for tossntrips was born out of a frustrating personal experience. While trying to book a one-week sailing tour in Croatia through a US-based company, they discovered just how complicated — and risky — the process could be. They spent hours trawling through countless websites, brochures, and magazines, transferred money to a company they weren’t even sure was legitimate, struggled to arrange transportation to and from the airport, and wandered through a foreign town just to locate the tour operator’s tiny office.

That experience sparked a realization: there had to be a better, safer, and simpler way to book travel experiences. And from that insight, tossntrips was born.

What is Tossntrip?

Tossntrip is the world’s leading platform for multi-day organized adventures, offering the largest selection of experiences across the globe. We connect travelers and travel agents with over 2,500 trusted tour operators, providing access to 50,000+ adventures in more than 160 countries — all through a seamless, user-friendly platform. With offices in North America, Europe, and Australia, our global team of travel experts is available 24/7 to support every stage of your journey, from booking to the adventure itself.

At Tossntrip, we’re committed to sustainable and responsible travel. Our platform allows travelers to view and offset the carbon footprint of their trips, and we collaborate closely with local operators to protect and empower communities that thrive through adventure tourism. Join us in exploring the world — responsibly, effortlessly, and adventurously.
